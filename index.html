Oh, hi there!

My name is Savannah Ostrowski. I majored in geomatics at the University of Waterloo in Canada. As an applied science combining physical geography with computer science, geomatics allowed me to find the world of programming. During my time in school, I interned at TheRedPin and Sequence working at a developer, and also at KBM Resources Group as a geomatics analyst.

I am interested in a lot of things. I love to learn. My current interests include, but are certainly not limited to: spatial data management and visualization, data modelling, and wearable tech.

In my free time, I like to go hiking in the mountains, do hot yoga, cook things like Okonomiyaki, ramen made from scratch, or a roast chicken dinner with all the fixings on a Tuesday night, watch movies from all genres (except horror), and travel as far and as much as I can with my husband (sometimes I even blog about it on The Perpetual Travellers).

I also really like meeting new people! If you too like meeting other humans, connect with me somewhere on the Internet.
